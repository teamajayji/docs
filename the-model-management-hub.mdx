title: The Model Management Hub
description: Under the Hood
---

The power and flexibility of the on-device AI in **aj** is made possible by a core architectural component: the Model Management Hub.

**Key Architectural Principles:**

*   **Load Once, Use Anywhere:** When you load a model into memory, it stays loaded and ready for immediate use by any feature in the app.
*   **Centralized State Management:** The Model Management Hub is the single source of truth for the state of all your on-device models.
*   **You're in Control:** From the model management screen, you have granular control over your models.
