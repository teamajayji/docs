The chat screen is the heart of your interaction with the AI in **aj**.

**Key Features:**

*   **Unified Interface:** The chat UI provides a consistent experience for all your AI interactions.
*   **Multi-Modal Communication:** The chat screen is not just for typing. It integrates on-device voice transcription.
*   **Persistent Conversations:** Every conversation is saved as a "chat session."
*   **Pre-configured Personas:** The host device can offer pre-configured "personas" with a specific model, a custom system prompt, and a curated set of tools.
