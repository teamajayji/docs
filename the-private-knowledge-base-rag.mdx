title: The Private Knowledge Base (RAG)
description: Your Secure Second Brain
---

Connect aj to your personal knowledge stores (like Google Drive or local folders) and turn them into a private, searchable database.

**How it Works:**

The app uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to find relevant information within your documents and uses it to provide accurate, context-aware answers to your questions.

**Key Benefits:**

*   **Truly Personal AI:** Ask questions about your own projects, find specific details in your notes, and get summaries of your documents.
*   **Complete Privacy:** The RAG feature operates on your local network, ensuring your data never leaves your control.
*   **Your Data, Your AI:** Unleash the power of AI on your own information to gain insights and boost your productivity.
