---
title: Local Network RAG
description: Your Personal AI Cloud
---

**aj** extends its on-device philosophy to your entire local network. You can designate one of your devices (e.g., your Mac or iPad) to act as a powerful, private AI "host," serving its models and knowledge bases to your other devices.

**How It Works:**

1.  **Host Your AI:** On your main device, you can start the "Hosted RAG Service."
2.  **Discover and Connect:** On another device, the app will automatically discover and list the available AI services being hosted on your network.
3.  **Chat Remotely:** Simply select one of the discovered services to start a conversation.

**Key Benefits:**

*   **Centralize Your Power:** Set up your most powerful device with your largest models and most comprehensive knowledge bases, and access them from anywhere on your local network.
*   **Zero Configuration:** The app uses Bonjour/mDNS to automatically discover services on your network.
*   **Private and Secure:** All communication happens exclusively on your local network.
*   **Flexible and Efficient:** This architecture allows you to use lightweight devices as thin clients, while the more computationally intensive work is handled by a more powerful host device.
