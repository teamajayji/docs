---
title: On-Device Translation
description: Fast, Secure, and Offline-Capable
---

In addition to transcription, **aj** leverages the power of on-device AI to provide fast, secure, and offline-capable audio translation.

**How It Works:**

Within the app's model testing and management screen, you'll find a simple toggle that lets you switch between "Transcribe" and "Translate" modes.

1.  **Select Translate Mode:** Simply activate the "Translate" toggle before you begin your recording.
2.  **Record Your Audio:** Speak in a different language.
3.  **Get an English Translation:** The on-device model will process the audio and will return the translated English text.

**A Stable, Parallel Architecture:**

To ensure the highest level of stability, the translation feature was built as a completely parallel and separate system from the core transcription functionality.

**Key Benefits:**

*   **Fast and Offline:** Translation happens entirely on your device.
*   **Private and Secure:** Your audio data never leaves your device.
*   **Seamless Experience:** The translation feature is integrated directly into the voice processing UI.
*   **Persistent History:** Your translation sessions are saved to your device.
