---
title: Craft Your Own AI with Personas
description: Reusable AI Configurations
---

The "Persona" feature in **aj** allows you to save and reuse complex AI configurations. A persona is like a recipe for an AI: it bundles together a base model, a custom system prompt, a specific RAG configuration, and a curated set of tools into a single, reusable entity.

**How It Works:**

You can create and manage your personas from a dedicated screen in the app. When you create a new persona, you can configure it with:

*   **A base model**
*   **A custom system prompt**
*   **A RAG configuration**
*   **A set of tools**

Once you've saved a persona, you can start a chat with it from the persona's detail screen.

**Key Benefits:**

*   **Reusable Configurations:** Personas allow you to save and quickly access your favorite AI configurations.
*   **Specialized Assistants:** You can create a stable of specialized AI assistants.
*   **Simplified Chat Experience:** When you start a chat with a persona, it's all pre-configured for you.
