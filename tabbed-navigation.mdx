---
title: Tabbed Navigation
description: A Desktop-Class Experience
---

The macOS version of **aj** is designed to feel right at home on your desktop, and its powerful tabbed navigation system is a core part of that experience.

**How It Works:**

Instead of forcing you to navigate back and forth between screens, the app allows you to open any main feature in its own separate tab. Each tab maintains its own independent state, including your scroll position and navigation history.

**Key Benefits:**

*   **True Multitasking:** Work on multiple tasks in parallel.
*   **Seamless Workflow:** The tabbed interface makes it easy to cross-reference information between different parts of the application.
*   **A Personalized Workspace:**
    *   **Customizable Sidebar:** You can enable or disable the items you see in the main navigation sidebar.
    *   **Favorites:** You can mark your most frequently used screens as "favorites."
