---
title: Getting Started
description: How to get started with aj.
---

1.  **Download the App:** Get the aj app for your iPhone/iPad and your Mac.
2.  **Set Up Your Devices:** Follow the in-app instructions to connect your devices.
3.  **Start Scribing:** Try out the Asynchronous Scribe to capture your first dictation.
4.  **Connect Your Knowledge Base:** Connect your Google Drive or a local folder to start querying your own data.

We're excited for you to experience the power and privacy of aj. Welcome aboard!
